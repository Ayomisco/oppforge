OppForge is building the infrastructure to fix this. We want to become how Web3 connects capital with talent.

Every ecosystem posts opportunities directly. Every builder has a live dashboard. Every grant becomes discoverable globally. On-chain reputation tracks application history. OppForge becomes the opportunity layer of Web3.

Today we're launching the MVP. But this is designed to be a venture-scale company solving a real infrastructure gap.
