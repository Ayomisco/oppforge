## Submission Requirements

### To Qualify (minimum bar):
1. **Use OppForge** — sign up, browse opportunities, use at least one AI feature (scoring, Forge workspace, or chat)
2. **Create content** — video (60+ seconds) OR written post (300+ words) OR X thread (5+ tweets)
3. **Post publicly** — must be posted on X, YouTube, TikTok, or a blog
4. **Tag @OppForge** and use #OppForgeChallenge
5. **Be honest** — authentic reactions. If something is broken, say it. Genuine feedback > fake hype.

### Bonus Points For:
- Showing a specific persona use case (bounty hunting, testnet farming, community manager searching, etc.)
- Using the AI Forge to generate a real proposal
- Comparing OppForge to manually searching Discord/Twitter
- Showing the platform to other people and recording their reaction
- High production quality
- Creative angles

## Maximizing $100 — Multiplier Tactics

### 1. Target Micro-Creators (100-5K followers)
Don't chase big influencers. Micro-creators in Web3:
- Actually need the money
- Will put in genuine effort for $40
- Have engaged niche audiences
- Are more likely to give honest reviews

### 2. Target Your Network First
- African Web3 communities (Web3Bridge, Blockchain UNN, Web3 Lagos)
- Nigerian/Kenyan/Ghanaian creators who cover Web3
- SuperTeam Nigeria members
- People who've engaged with your tweets before

### 3. Cross-Post the Challenge
Post the bounty announcement in:
- [ ] Your X account
- [ ] @OppForge account
- [ ] Web3Bridge Telegram/Discord
- [ ] Developer DAO Discord
- [ ] Any other communities you're in
- [ ] LinkedIn

### 4. Make the Content Evergreen
After the challenge:
- Compile all creator content into a "Social Proof" thread
- Use the best videos on the OppForge website
- Use quotes from reviews in future marketing
- Screenshot positive comments for ad creatives

### 5. Turn Winners Into Ambassadors
- Offer winning creators ongoing small bounties ($10-20) for future content
- Give them early access to new features
- Build a small creator squad (3-5 people) who get perks for ongoing content
- This seeds your ambassador program for when you have real funding
