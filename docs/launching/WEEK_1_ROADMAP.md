## Key Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low signups | Pivot ad spend to best-performing persona. DM more people directly. |
| Scrapers break | Have manual curation as backup. Keep curated scraper loaded. |
| No creator bounty entries | DM people directly with $40 prize amount. Lower the bar. |
| Negative feedback | Address publicly and fix fast. Transparency builds trust. |
| Platform downtime | Monitor Railway. Have rollback ready. |
| No ecosystem responses to DMs | Follow up. Try different contacts. Tag in public as last resort. |
