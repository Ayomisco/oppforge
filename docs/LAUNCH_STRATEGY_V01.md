> **Updated**: March 7, 2026  
> **Platform**: app.oppforge.xyz  
> **Target**: Web3 builders, hackathon participants, bounty hunters, grant seekers

## 1. Core Positioning

**The pitch**: "Scouting Web3 opportunities is a full-time job. OppForge's AI does it for you while you sleep — finds them, scores them, and helps you win them."

**Differentiators**:
- Not another aggregator — it actively helps you *win*, not just discover
- AI workspace (Forge) generates real proposal drafts and strategies
- On-chain reputation layer via smart contracts — trustless hunter profiles
- Built by a builder, for builders — understands the actual workflow
